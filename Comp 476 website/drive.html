About the Paramount Cult Classic

Nacho Libre is a 2006 sports comedy film directed by Jared Hess(Director of Napolean Dynamite) and starring Jack Black as Ignacio, a monastery cook who secretly becomes a luchador (Nacho) to earn money for orphaned children. Set in Oaxaca Mexico, this quirky cult classic blends outlandish humor with heartfelt moments as Nacho battles professional wrestlers while keeping his identity hidden from the monastery. The film's distinctive visual style,memorable catchphrases, and Jack Black's committed performance have earned it a dedicated fanbase. With its unique soundtrack featuring both Mexican folk music (ft. Mister Loco) and original compositions, Nacho Libre has secured its place in pop culture as a wholesome celebration of wrestling, faith, and following one's dreams, making it into this "Project Web Page".
